Campaign duration

Choose the optimal time frame for your campaign, as this will set the pace for your fundraising efforts. Once launched, you won't be able to modify this duration, so it's crucial to select wisely. Consider factors such as the complexity of your project, your target audience's responsiveness, and the momentum you aim to build. A well-thought-out campaign duration can create a sense of urgency, motivating backers to support your cause within the allotted time. Take your time to decide, as this decision will shape the trajectory of your crowdfunding journey.
