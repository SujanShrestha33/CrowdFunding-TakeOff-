Who can pledge on TakeOff?

While millions of enthusiasts peruse TakeOff each week, your primary supporters will likely be your existing network of friends, followers, and communities. They'll be your initial champions and vital advocates for spreading the word about your project.

How much effort does it take to run a project on TakeOff?

Running a project on TakeOff can be both exhilarating and demanding, contingent upon the scope and complexity of your endeavor. Expect an intense flurry of activity in the initial days post-launch as you engage with your community, address inquiries from potential backers, and more. Towards the project's conclusion, you may find yourself rallying your social circles to ensure you reach your funding goal. Projects may take on a life of their own, demanding additional time for reward creation and fulfillment. If you haven't pondered over the fulfillment aspect yet, fret not – we've got you covered.
