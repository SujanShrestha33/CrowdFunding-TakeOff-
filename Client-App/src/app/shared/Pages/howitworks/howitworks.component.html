How It Works

1

Start Your Campaigns

Get started by creating a campaign on TakeOff. Describe your idea, set a funding goal, and choose a deadline.

2

Share with Your Community

Spread the word about your campaign to your friends, family, and followers. Use social media and email to reach potential backers.

3

Receive Backing

Once your campaign is live, backers can pledge their support. Keep them updated with campaign updates and milestones.

4

Reach Your Goal

If your campaign reaches its funding goal by the deadline, you'll receive the funds to bring your idea to life.

5

Receive Funds

Receive funds from Takeoff administration when your campaign reaches the goal.

6

Fulfill Rewards

Fulfill your promises to backers by delivering rewards as promised. Keep them informed throughout the process.
